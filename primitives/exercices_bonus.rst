-------------

b) :math:` \int {\frac{1}{(x-3)^2}-\frac{4}{(x+2)}}dx`

c) :math:` \int {\frac{x}{\sqrt {1-x^2}}}dx `

d) :math:` \int {\frac{1}{x\ln x}}dx`

e) :math:` \int {x^2\ln x}dx`

f) :math:`\int {\frac{\sin x}{\cos^3 x}}dx`
